SIAM/CAIMS 2025 Annual Meeting

Student Days Orientation Scavenger Hunt

Use any resources you like to find answers to the following questions! Groups who score the highest win a prize.
  1. Who is the first invited plenary speaker on Monday?
  2. Who authored the abstract for the minitutorial on Wednesday?
  3. What time is the last coffee break of the conference?
  4. When does the Exhibit Hall first open?
  5. Where will Journeys of Black Mathematicians documentary screening and discussion take place?
  6. How many SIAM student chapters are there?
  7. Who sponsors the M3 Challenge?
  8. How much does your student membership cost if your university is an academic member of SIAM, or you are a member of a SIAM student chapter, or you are nominated by a non-student SIAM member?
  9. How many SIAGs can student members join for free?
  10. How many SIAGs are there to choose from?
  11. Who is the current SIAM President?
  12. How many countries do SIAM members come from?
  13. How much does a subscription to SIAM News, SIAM Review (e-access) and SIAM Unwrapped cost for student members?
  14. How many times per year is SIAM News distributed in print?
  15. What is the student discount on SIAM books?
  16. Which SIAM programs are geared toward graduate students?
  17. How much does it cost to join a geographic SIAM section?
  18. How many peer-reviewed research journals does SIAM publish?
  19. What does it take to win the "Red Sock Award"? Do you actually get a red sock?
  20. How much does a poutine cost at Le Grand PoutineFest in Old Port, running July 27 through August 3?
